GATE – Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering -It is a national level examination which gets conducted each year. The main reason of conducting this examination is to test the aptitude and subject knowledge of the applicant and on the basis of that; they get admission in Engineering PG programmes.

How to get prepared for GATE ?

The first step to start with preparation is to know the syllabus of GATE. Set a time table according to the syllabus you will have to cover. It is not enough to set a time table for your study but more important is to follow that time table.

1. How to start with preparation GATE examination is one of the high level examinations in India, hence the candidates required lot of hard work to get success in that. It is very important that the candidates have transparent understanding of the concepts. If they focus on mugging up the things and scoring high then it going to be a tough time for them in the examination. Understanding the concepts is even more important than mugging up the facts. Hence concentrate more on clearing the concepts rather than mugging up the topics.

2. How much time can you give? If you are a student it will not be difficult for you to manage time for GATE preparation because you are already in flow of study. If you have done with your studied and interested in GATE then you can plan your study accordingly. But if you are working then you might feel it little tough to get time for study. If you can manage 2-3 hours every day consistently then you can prepare well for GATE. All the interested candidates in GATE should start their study from now onwards seriously.

3. Arrangement of study material The detailed syllabus and previous years’ question papers gives you an idea about the examination pattern & topics to cover. Arrangement of good study material for the preparation of GATE is another important step to be taken out. Collection of study material will depend on the subject you choose for GATE . There are 22 papers for GATE and you have to choose any one out of these 22. The candidates have to mention their choice during application procedure. The list of GATE subjects are as follows-
Life science Engineering sciences Textile eng.&fiber sciences
Production & industrial eng. Physics Metallurgic eng.
Mining eng. Mechanical eng. Mathematics
Instrumentation eng. Geology & geophysics Ecology & evolution
Electrical eng. Electronics & communication eng. Chemistry
Computer sciences & IT Chemical eng. Civil eng.
Biotechnology Architecture & planning Agricultural eng.
Aerospace eng.
The candidates have to choose the subject which they have studied in graduation or pursuing. The general aptitude section is common and mandatory for all the students. The duration of the examination will be 3 hours. There will be total 65 questions of MCQ type in the question paper and the total marks will be 100.

4. Preparation for general aptitude This section includes verbal ability and numerical ability. This section is common for all the candidates. In this section English Grammar plays a vital role. If you have a good command over English Grammar then it will be little easy to attempt.

5. Time require for the preparation First of all think how much time you needed for preparation and how much time is left. Now make a study plan accordingly and try to stick into that plan. Once you have decided to apply for GATE 2017, you should start planning your preparation right there. Accurate time table is very important to navigate your study into right direction.

6. How much making notes are helpful? This is a kind of traditional formula to get prepared for any examination. When you make your own notes, it put great impression to your mind. This technique helps the students to remember the topics much easier. The candidates are advised to rely on their own notes rather than their friends’ notes. Hence, make habit of making your own notes. Learn and write the topics.
