GATE Registration:

The candidates can do their initial registration by giving their valid Email ID, mobile No. and a GOAPS(GATE Online Application Processing System ) password (which you get at the time of enrolment).You will get a GOAPS Enrolment ID once you done with successful enrolment. It is advised to the candidates to note down the enrolment ID and password for further use. The candidates can start their application procedure by making below mentioned documents ready-

 Personal details of the candidates  Address of the candidate for communication  Details of the qualifying degrees.  GATE papers  Examination centre for GATE  Scanned photograph and signature  Details of ID proof of the candidate The candidates are advised to fill their application form carefully to avoid mistakes. You also need to mention the mode of payment while filling the application form.

Application fee:

The candidates can pay their application fee either online or offline. The candidates can also pay the fee through Net Banking in online mode and in offline mode the fee has to be paid through e-challan.

You can pay the application fee from any bank of your choice in case of Net Banking but in offline mode you have to pay the fee only through branches of Syndicate Bank or State Bank of India. You can pay the fee after two days of online registration.

The candidates have to send the hard copy of their application form along with all the required documents to the Zonal office of the GATE prior last date. The applications have to be sent through registered or speed post.

1. All Supporting documents should be sent online only. No hard copy will be accepted 2. Payments have to be made through debit/ATM cards, credit cards or internet banking and e-challan only
